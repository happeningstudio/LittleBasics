Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order? ▾
Browse the catalog, add items to your bag by selecting size and color, then hit "Submit Order Draft." We'll receive your request, confirm what's available, and send you payment details via DM. Easy!
What is the bundle price? ▾
Add 5 or more items to your bag and the bundle price automatically applies to eligible items. The bundle price is shown on each product card. Mix and match any styles!
Do you have a size chart? ▾
No — but for good reason. Every child is unique. We offer a measuring service upon request, using the actual items you'll receive. No more guessing games.
Where do the items come from? Do you sew them yourself? ▾
Can't sew a stitch — so you're safe from my creations! Instead, I carefully source from certified vendors who specialize in quality baby and kids clothing. Every item is personally reviewed before it makes it to the catalog. Questions? Just DM me!
Why can't I purchase directly from the catalog? ▾
Two reasons, both in your favor. First, I sell across a few platforms and manually confirming each order means I never accidentally sell something that's already gone — no surprise cancellations on your end. Second, skipping a built-in checkout means skipping the platform fees that get baked into most online shop prices. That one extra step of sending a message is how I keep prices honest. I think that's a trade worth making.
Can I pick up locally or view items in person? ▾
Yes! We can schedule an in-person viewing Monday–Friday, 10am–3pm on most days. Contact-free pickup is also available 24/7. Select "Local Pickup" in your bag and we'll coordinate the details via DM.
How does shipping work? ▾
We ship across the US. Estimated shipping is calculated in your bag based on your ZIP code. California orders typically arrive in 2–4 days. Final shipping cost is confirmed when we finalize your order. All shipped orders include tracking!
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept Venmo, Zelle, PayPal, and cash (for local pickup). Payment details are sent after we confirm your order.
What if my size is sold out? ▾
Little Basics restocks monthly! Follow us on Instagram @little_basics_la for the latest restock announcements and new arrival updates.
Can I return or exchange? ▾
Returns and exchanges are accepted within 2 weeks of purchase for unused, brand new items. Buyers are responsible for return shipping. If you have an issue, DM us — we're always happy to work out a solution together.
How do I know if my order went through? ▾
After submitting your order draft, we receive your order instantly. We'll reach out via Instagram or Facebook DM within 24 hours to confirm availability and send payment details. No extra steps needed from you!
Do you do wholesale? ▾
Yes! I want these clothes to reach as many families as possible. DM us for more details.
Who are you? Why are you selling children's clothes? ▾
A mom who got tired of choosing between cute and affordable — so I did something about it. I spent months researching and testing vendors to find cotton clothing that's genuinely safe, comfortable, and won't break the bank. The response surprised me — strangers started stopping us to ask where my son's outfits were from. That's when I knew other families needed this too. Little Basics exists for you.
